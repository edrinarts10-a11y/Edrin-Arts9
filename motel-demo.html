◆ Our Story

A Landmark Since 1962

Built when Route 66 was still the spine of the American dream, Sunset Strip Motel has welcomed road-weary travelers, honeymooners, wanderers, and writers for over six decades.

We haven't forgotten what a motel is supposed to feel like — neon buzzing in the evening air, clean sheets, an ice machine around the corner, and a diner across the road with coffee that keeps you going another hundred miles.
